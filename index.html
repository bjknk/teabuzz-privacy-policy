Teabuzz Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Teabuzz by Blackish Technologies collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Registration: Email, username, password, and phone number
  • Profile Information: Name, profile picture, school, location, bio
  • User Content: Posts, comments, messages, and uploaded media
  • Usage Data: App interactions, device type, operating system, and analytics data
  • Location Data: (Only if enabled by you) for location-based features

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve our services
  • To personalize your experience and content
  • To enable communication and community features
  • To maintain security and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To communicate updates, support, and important notices

3. Information Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) under confidentiality agreements.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect users and the platform.
  • Some profile information may be visible to other users depending on your privacy settings.

4. Data Security

  • We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit.
  • We regularly review and update our security practices.
  • No system is completely secure; users should use strong passwords and remain cautious.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data
  • Delete your account at any time
  • Control your privacy settings within the app
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

You can request account or data deletion by contacting us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

Teabuzz is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If such data is identified, it will be removed promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via the app or email.
